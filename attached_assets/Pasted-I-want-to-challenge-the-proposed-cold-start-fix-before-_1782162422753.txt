I want to challenge the proposed cold-start fix before approving implementation.

Problem:
Fresh onboarded users who provide intake signals, for example Mystery + Thriller, but have no library/import history, are currently being classified as confidenceMode='thin' instead of confidenceMode='sparse_onboarding'. That causes coldStartAdjacent quota to be 0 instead of 3, which likely weakens the first recommendation experience.

You diagnosed the root cause as:

* tasteProfile.ts boosts a fresh intake user to tier 1
* recRequest.ts passes the boosted profile.tier into confidenceModeForTier
* tier 1 maps to thin
* therefore intakeBoosted users lose sparse_onboarding behavior

Your proposed fix is:

* add unboosted rawTier to TasteProfile
* keep boosted tier where it is still needed
* pass rawTier into confidenceModeForTier
* bump rcv8 to rcv9 and csrp2 to csrp3
* add an end-to-end validator
