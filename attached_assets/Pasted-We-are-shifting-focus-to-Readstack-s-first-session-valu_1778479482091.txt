We are shifting focus to Readstack’s first-session value loop.

Do not implement anything yet. This first pass is an audit and product/technical planning pass only.

Product goal:
A new user should quickly feel that Readstack understands their reading taste, gives them a useful next-read shortlist, explains why those books fit, and visibly learns from their actions.

Core loop we are improving:
Import/add books → understand taste → recommend better next reads → explain why → user acts → system learns.

Strategic framing:
Readstack should not feel like a generic book tracker or a generic recommendation feed. It should feel like a personal reading decision system: something that helps a reader choose their next book with more confidence based on their taste, history, mood, and feedback.
