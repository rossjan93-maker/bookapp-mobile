## 2. Separate durable taste from current intent

Define a clean distinction between:

### Durable taste
Long-running reader identity:
- genres
- repeated authors
- recurring book traits
- imported history
- ratings
- finishes
- DNFs later
- repeated preferences

### Current intent
What the reader wants now:
- escape
- challenge
- comfort
- fast pacing
- depth
- lighter tone
- darker tone
- continuation
- palette cleanser
- short read
- not-right-now

Explain where current code currently mixes these concepts, if it does.
