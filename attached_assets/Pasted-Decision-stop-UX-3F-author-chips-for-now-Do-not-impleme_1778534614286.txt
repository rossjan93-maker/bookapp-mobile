Decision: stop UX-3F author chips for now.

Do not implement Option A.

Reason:
The preflight surfaced that reader_preferences.favorite_authors already flows into tasteProfile.liked_authors. If we write intake authors into favorite_authors, those self-reported authors may silently influence recommender behavior and author-loyalty explanations. That violates the trust boundary for this batch.

We should not treat self-reported intake authors as derived liked_authors unless the product language and model behavior explicitly support that.

Please do not make code changes for UX-3F.

Instead, run a small audit-only follow-up.
