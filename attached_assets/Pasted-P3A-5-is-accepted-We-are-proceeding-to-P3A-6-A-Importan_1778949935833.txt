## 3. Acceptance criteria

For each scenario, verify:

1. Top cards have at least one reason when eligible evidence exists.
2. No empty-reasons regression.
3. No reason cites a stated key the user did not state.
4. No specific trait / subject / genre claim appears unless composer evidence supports it.
5. No quality_reliability-only book receives personal-taste language.
6. Retrieval-only candidates do not receive causal fit explanations.
7. Negative contributions surface only as caution/risk, not positive reason.
8. Ranking/order remains observably stable versus flag OFF.
9. P2 stated reservation path remains unaffected.
10. No banned phrasing appears.
