### avoidance_conflict
Should be negative or neutral only.
Requires:

- do not turn this into positive reasoning
- do not hard-exclude yet

### not_right_now_risk
Should be negative or neutral only.
Requires:
- session intent suggests lighter / low-burden / palette-cleanser type need
- book is dark, long, dense, or otherwise conflicting
