We approve the diagnosis:
- The ~10s “Almost there… Setting up your reading life” delay is a confirmed app bug.
- The root cause is async Supabase-dependent warm-boot work running inside the `onAuthStateChange` SIGNED_IN callback in `app/_layout.tsx`.
- That creates the documented supabase-js auth-lock deadlock pattern:
  - the SIGNED_IN listener runs while the auth lock is still held
  - `checkOnboardingCompleted(...)` performs a same-client PostgREST query
  - the query waits on the lock
  - gotrue emits the auth-token lock warning
  - the first timeout fires at ~10s
  - only then does onboarding routing proceed
