## Honest limits of `wem` and `lfa`

Both are Pattern-A pure derivations (runbook §4):
- `wem` can **overstate** ejectability (low-leaning candidates in 11–25 may themselves be low-scoring on durable taste).
- `wem` can **understate** Phase-2 impact (it cannot see candidates retrieval dropped before slot 25).
- A `lf === 'mismatch'` verdict on a book with `int = unknown/unk` AND `wt = unknown/unk` is a classifier miss, not a real mismatch. The `classifier_miss_rate` aggregate above flags this.
